Progressions and summations

Progressions

Arithmetic progression: $S_n=a+\(a+d\)+\(a+2d\)+...$
$+\[a+\(n-1\)d\]$
$={n}/{2}\[2a+\(n-1\)d\]$
$={n}/{2}\(a+l\)$
Geometric progression: $S_n=a+ar+ar^2+...+ar^{n-1}$
$=a{1-r^n}/{1-r}$
$S_∞={a}/{1-r} \text" " (|r|<1)$
Arithmetic mean: $\ov x_a={1}/{n}\(x_1+x_2+...+x_n\)$
Geometric mean: $\ov x_g=\(x_1⋅x_2⋅x_3⋅...⋅x_n\)^{1/n}$
Harmonic mean: $\ov x_h=n\({1}/{x_1}+{1}/{x_2}+{1}/{x_3}+...{1}/{x_n}\)^{-1}$
Relative mean magnitudes: $\ov x_a ≥ \ov x_g ≥\ov x_h \text" if " x_i>0 \text" for all " i$

where

$$n$$number of terms$$r$$common ratio
$$S_n$$sum of $n$ successive terms
$$a$$first term$$<...>_a$$arithmetic mean
$$d$$common difference$$<...>_g$$geometric mean
$$l$$last term$$<...>_h$$harmonic mean

Summations

$∑↙{i=1}↖n i={n(n+1)}/{2}$
$∑↙{i=1}↖n i^2={n(n+1)(2n+1)}/{6}$
$∑↙{i=1}↖n i^3={n^2(n+1)^2}/{4}$
Summations formulas$∑↙{i=1}↖n i^4={n}/{30}(n+1)(2n+1)(3n^2+3n-1)$
$∑↙{i=1}↖∞ {(-1)^{i+1}}/{i}=1-{1}/{2}+{1}/{3}-{1}/{4}+...=\ln 2$
$∑↙{i=1}↖∞ {(-1)^{i+1}}/{2i-1}=1-{1}/{3}+{1}/{5}-{1}/{7}+...={π}/{4}$
$∑↙{i=1}↖∞ {1}/{i^2}=1+{1}/{4}+{1}/{9}+{1}/{16}+...={π^2}/{6}$

where $i$ is dummy integer
